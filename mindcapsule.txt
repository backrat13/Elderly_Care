Your stack now has three genuinely defensible moats that a corporate competitor can't easily replicate. The smartest near-term framing is "wellness monitoring" rather than "medical device" — your system informs family and suggests when to call 911, rather than autonomously diagnosing or treating. HIPAA compliance is achievable with your local-first architecture since you're not transmitting PHI to the cloud, but you should document that explicitly in your data architecture from day one for when you seek care home partnerships.
